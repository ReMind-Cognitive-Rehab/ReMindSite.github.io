Privacy Policy

ReMind respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user information. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when using the ReMind application.

Information We Collect

ReMind may collect performance-related data such as game scores, reaction times, accuracy, completed trials, and other cognitive exercise results. If wearable integration is enabled, ReMind may also use health-related data such as heart rate or heart rate variability.

How We Use Information

Collected information is used to display results, track user progress, improve cognitive exercises, and support adaptive difficulty features. Data may also help improve future versions of the application.

Data Sharing

ReMind does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties. Any exported data is controlled by the user.

Data Security

ReMind takes reasonable steps to protect user information. However, no method of digital storage or transmission is completely secure.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@remindapp.com
